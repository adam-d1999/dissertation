CHANNEL 4 HEADQUARTERS

Author - Tom Stevenson

Date - October 30, 2019

Channel 4 Headquarters (London)

Channel 4 Headquarters in London reached out us to SureGRP, to supply and install bespoke high visual stair nosing in front of their building. The aim was to provide a warning system for the visually impaired, so it is easier to identify the edge of the step and walk in and out of the building safely.

The clients required our UV stable yellow nosing which complies with DDA and British regulations, to be supplied and fitted by our in-house installation team. The project installation was completed within 2 days, providing Channel 4 with a fast effective visual and anti-slip solution for the edge of their steps.

FASTAC TWO-PART MOISTURE TOLERANT ADHESIVE

£48.00

FasTac Two Part Moisture Tolerant Adhesive is a solvent– free, moisture tolerant, epoxy gap filling adhesive. Which provides excellent bond strength with non-shrink, non-slump properties. The moisture tolerant adhesive is ideal for bonding polyurethane tactile tiles to steel, concrete and asphalt.

Benefits

Moisture Tolerant

Solvent-free System

Excellent Bonding Strength

No primer

Excellent adhesion to a wide-range of construction surfaces

Non-shrink properties

Adheres to damp surfaces

Fast-curing

Mixing Ratio

Full packs only

Curing Time

1 hour @ 20°C—do not apply below 3°C as the cure time will be greatly extended.

Note

One tub of adhesive can be used to lay 8-10 tiles.

Storage

FasTac Adhesive should be stored at warm temperatures. If stored in cold conditions the containers should be warmed prior to mixing. This will greatly aid mixing and application.

Mixing

FasTac Adhesive is a 2-part adhesive. Both full packs should be used to give the correct ratio. The adhesive should be mixed thoroughly. Mix at a slow speed for 2-3 minutes with a power tool mixer and mixing paddle until even in colour and consistency is reached.

System Applications

The mixed Epoxy Adhesive should be applied using a serrated trowel and evenly spread over the area to receive the tiles. Firmly press the tiles into position, working from the centre to the edges. Remove excess adhesive and tool to a smooth finish. All equipment should be cleaned immediately after use.

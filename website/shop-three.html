Description

Our SureGRP Solution kit is an ideal option to dress concrete, steel & timber surfaces as well as uneven & stubborn walkways with an instant high-visual, anti-slip covering – Simply adhere with a roller and add the grit!

Our Anti-Slip Solution is complied of a formulated adhesive, implemented with a quartz-grit – which is available in your select choice of colour (RAL colour scheme).

SureGRP solution includes the distinctive SureGRP high slip resistant anti-slip safety surface, a colour you can specify (we recommend yellow, grey, black or blue) – which gives a firm foot grip in the most severe conditions.

A full colour range is available in a wide range of colour schemes to suit individual locations (E.G. Food Processing Area Standard – Grey).

The slip resistant, quartz-grit solution is a quick & simple fix, with a permanent basis. It’s a single component moisture cure polyurethane developed for application by rollercoater.

The strong and hard-wearing products are manufactured under quality-controlled conditions comprising of a resistant composite base and a quarts grit surface. As a SureGRP standard, these products are slip and wear tested.

We have found the solution to be ideal for use in industrial areas, such as manufacturing areas and loading bays on factory floors, amongst so many additional areas.

Expect little down-time too, after laying the solution you will see it set immediately and be ready for walking on within the hour, it’s simple to install using our instruction manuals and simple videos explaining the recommended methods available to view below.

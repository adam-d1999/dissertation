About Our Company

SureGRP are leaders within GRP/ Fibreglass Anti-Slip Flooring Surface manufacture.

Our business aims to be able to offer exactly what the customer requires – we manufacture all of our products in-house at our factory in Shropshire, West Midlands, UK.

As a small family business, we’re growing year on year and are always working on prestigious exciting projects including at palaces, stadiums & across the country in many councils. You can check some of our case-studies from our previous projects throughout the site.

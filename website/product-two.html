ANTI-SLIP STRUCTURAL STAIR TREADS

SureGRP offer a unique structural anti-slip load-bearing stair tread. Designed to provide a low maintanence alternative to traditional load-bearing wooden stair treads. Our structural stair treads offer an ideal solution for renewal or individual tread replacement projects.

Load Bearing Structural Anti-Slip Stair Treads

Our products offer a corrosion and weather resistant, non-conductive and require next to no maintenance alternative to traditional load bearing stair treads. Making them ideally suitable to electrified rail environments and for use on footfall footbridges.

Available in 65mm or 90mm thicknesses and span up to 2.1 metres.

Key Features

SureGRP Structural Stair Treads and landings can be easily fixed into position thanks to their interlocking construction.

Our Structural Stair Tread requires very little maintenance due to its excellent impact and load-bearing characteristics.

After testing by the British Standards, our Structural Stair Tread surface was found to be classified as an enhanced slip resistant product with an expected life span of 25+ years.

The Tread comes with UV stable visual sight lines to comply to with DDA regulation. Sight lines are available in your choice of colour.

Applications

Platforms, Walkways, Stairs, Maintenance bays, Fire escapes, Roof walkways, Wash bays, Ramps, Bridges

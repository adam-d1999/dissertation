Anti-Slip Stair Treads

Anti Slip GRP Stair Treads are designed to fit over existing stairs, providing a cost-effective abrasive anti slip solution for slippery stairs, thus improving safety in potentially hazardous areas. The product is ideal for industrial use or in areas with high footfall and worn steps. Offering a long-term solution due to a specialised quartz grit impregnated into a resin surface.

Our non-slip stair treads can be used in a range of applications from fire escapes to staircases in large train stations. SureGRP utilise isophthalic resin incorporated with fibreglass matting to increase durability and overall safety.

All anti slip stair treads are manufactured in the UK to the required size, so no needs for cutting or wastage. The tread covers have a high visual nosing incorporated into the profile which is usually in a contrasting colour to meet DDA regulations. The nosing dimensions are 55mm x 55mm as a standard to meet current building regulations but can be made to specific requirements.

Square Nosed Stair Treads

Our most popular SureGRP square nosed stair treads are designed with a 90 degree angle to install directly onto most traditional staircase.

Round Nosed Stair Treads

The SureGRP round nosed stair treads are designed to fit over existing stair treads to give improving anti-slip properties and step edge visibility.

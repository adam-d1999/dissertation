ANTI-SLIP STAIR NOSING

SureGRP offer two main types of GRP anti-slip stair nosing profiles; square and rake back profiles. The nosings provide a high visual guideline which complies with British and DDA regulations.

Anti-Slip Stair Nosings

All nosings are manufactured by hand and manufactured to your required dimension. The most common nosing specification is usually 55mm x 55mm x by your width (But can be Bespoke manufactured).

Styles of Stair Tread Nosing Sections SureGRP Offer:

SureGRP offer two types of GRP anti-slip stair nosing profiles; 90 degree angle and rake back profiles.

Our most popular SureGRP square nosed stair treads are designed with a 90 degree angle to install directly onto most traditional staircase.

Our Rake Back Nosing profiles are designed to be fitted onto stairs which are mostly found in new builds on concrete stairs with a rake of 83 degrees.

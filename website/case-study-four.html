SUREGRP DONATE NON-SLIP TO HISTORIC SITE

Author - Tom Stevenson

Date - August 8, 2019

SureGRP donate Non-Slip to Historic Site

SureGRP Ltd have donated £25,000 worth of Non-Slip flooring to build a safe walkway around the Lochnagar Crater in France.

Adrian Stevenson said “This site is a major part of British history and attracts thousands of visitors a year. We are pleased to be able to help with this project and will be donating the materials which would normally cost £25,000 free of charge. Once the work is completed we will know those visitors will be safe.”

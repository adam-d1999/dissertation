THV PATRICIA HELICOPTER PAD

Author - Tom Stevenson

Date - October 31, 2019

THV Patricia Helicopter Pad

At SureGRP we pride ourselves on providing anti-slip solutions nationwide. In 2017, we accepted to project to manufacture and supply approx 105 square meters of 6mm thick SureGRP anti-slip surface flooring to replace the Helipad covering on the THV Patricia Vessel.

The new flooring will incorporate the landing `H` and landing circle, all edges scribed and marked to match the existing covering.The colours were replicated to match the existing markings and the anchor points cut out where required. The flooring was supplied in approx 30 x 2m x 2m boards, which were delivered to site stacked and palletised.

The flooring and tools were craned onto the deck using the boats crane boom due to the restricted access onto the ship.

SureGRP anti-slip sheets have a design life of over 25 years, anti-slip and load-bearing characteristics making it the ideal solution for helicopter pad flooring panels.
